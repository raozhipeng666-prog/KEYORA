痛点与洞察

Problem & Insight

通勤/短途/轻徒步：水具易磕碰、易误开、背包内滚动；现有瓶套缺少“安全锁止”。

Bottles get scuffed, open accidentally or roll in bags; generic sleeves lack safety locking.

解决方案

Solution

一体式锁体与袋口结构；半包覆安装座与受力肋；导向斜面、反挑窄缝、静音缓冲与可视确认。

Integrated lock & mouth; semi-wrap mount & ribs; guiding ramps, anti-shim slit, silent buffer, visual confirmation.

Ningbo/Yiwu/Yongkang cluster; 7–10d proto; 10–25d semi/full custom with fixtures & SOP.
